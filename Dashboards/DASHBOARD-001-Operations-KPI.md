## 💡 Why This Dashboard Exists

Operations teams generate enormous amounts of data every 
single day. Shipment statuses, task completion rates, 
vendor performance scores, budget burn rates; it is all 
there but it is scattered across spreadsheets, project 
boards, and email threads that nobody has time to dig through.

This dashboard template was built to solve one specific 
problem: giving operations leaders a single, clear view 
of what is happening right now and whether it is on track 
or needs attention. No digging, no manual compilation, 
no waiting until Friday's status report to find out 
something went sideways on Tuesday.

The philosophy behind it is simple. If a metric is not 
actionable, it does not belong on the dashboard. Every 
KPI here exists because it tells a decision maker something 
they can actually do something about.

## SECTION 1: Operational Efficiency

These metrics tell you whether your core processes are 
running the way they are designed to run. Green means 
the process is healthy. Amber means it needs attention 
before it becomes a problem. Red means it already is one.

| KPI | Target | Current | Status | Trend |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| On-Time Task Completion Rate | ≥ 90% | — | 🟢 🟡 🔴 | ↑ ↓ → |
| Process Adherence Rate | ≥ 95% | — | 🟢 🟡 🔴 | ↑ ↓ → |
| SOP Compliance Rate | 100% | — | 🟢 🟡 🔴 | ↑ ↓ → |
| Average Task Resolution Time | ≤ 24 hours | — | 🟢 🟡 🔴 | ↑ ↓ → |
| Escalation Rate | ≤ 5% | — | 🟢 🟡 🔴 | ↑ ↓ → |
| Automation Adoption Rate | ≥ 80% | — | 🟢 🟡 🔴 | ↑ ↓ → |

**How to Read Status Indicators**

🟢 Green: At or above target; no action required  
🟡 Amber: Within 10% of target; monitor closely  
🔴 Red: More than 10% below target; immediate action required  

**How to Read Trend Arrows**

↑ Improving compared to last reporting period  
↓ Declining compared to last reporting period  
→ Stable; no significant change

## SECTION 3: Team and Resource Metrics

People are the most important operational asset and 
also the most frequently overlooked one in KPI dashboards. 
These metrics exist to make sure the team is not burning 
out, that capacity is being used effectively, and that 
training and development are not falling behind.

**Capacity and Workload**

| Metric | Target | Current | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Team Capacity Utilization | 70% to 85% | — | Below 70% is underutilized; above 85% is burnout risk |
| Open Task Age (avg days) | ≤ 5 days | — | Tasks older than 10 days flagged for review |
| Cross-Training Coverage | ≥ 2 per role | — | No single point of failure on critical functions |
| Overtime Hours (weekly) | ≤ 5% of total | — | Consistent overtime indicates capacity problem |

**Training and Development**

| Metric | Target | Current | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Security Awareness Training Completion | 100% | — | Required annually; tracked by IT Security |
| SOP Certification Rate | 100% | — | All staff certified on SOPs relevant to their role |
| Professional Development Hours | ≥ 8 hrs/quarter | — | Includes certifications, courses, and workshops |

## 🎨 Visualization Guidance

This template is designed to be implemented across 
multiple platforms depending on the audience and use case:

**For internal weekly team reviews**
Build in Google Sheets using conditional formatting 
to auto-color cells green, amber, and red based on 
target thresholds. Share via Google Drive link.

**For leadership and executive reporting**
Export data into Canva branded dashboard template 
or use Gamma to generate a clean presentation version. 
Distribute as PDF or shareable link.

**For live project management visibility**
Mirror key metrics into a Notion dashboard page 
using linked databases so the team has real-time 
visibility without waiting for the weekly report.

**For formal board or client reporting**
Use PowerPoint with the organization's branded template. 
Charts generated in Excel and pasted as images for 
clean, professional presentation.

## 🔐 Security and Data Handling

Dashboard data contains financial figures, vendor 
performance information, and risk status details 
that are classified as internal and confidential.

Distribution restricted to authorized personnel only; 
Google Sheets dashboard access managed via role-based 
sharing settings; exported PDFs for executive reporting 
password protected for sensitive financial data; 
Canva and Gamma outputs shared via link with view-only 
access; and dashboard data never shared in public 
channels or with unauthorized third parties.
